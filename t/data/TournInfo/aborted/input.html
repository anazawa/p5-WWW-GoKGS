January KGS+ Tournament, Asian/European daytime 2k and weaker division (Aborted)

Welcome to the January KGS Plus tournament!

Swiss style, Simultaneous Schedule. This tournament uses the Swiss pairing system. The time settings are 37 minutes Main time plus 10 Byo yomi periods of 10 seconds (37m + 10s x 10) , 1 round every 90min. Five rounds total, 3 rounds on the first day, 2 rounds on the second day. Prizes are given out every round, so even if you don't think you can win your division, you still have a chance at getting a prize!

Important: Conditions for tournament entry:

  • The 2K and weaker Handicap division is KGS Plus members only.
  • 50 non-KGS Plus members will be allowed to join the Open division. KGS Plus members are always welcome!
  • Players who have been previously disqualified from a KGS tournament are not allowed.
  • People who do not fullfill those conditions but register anyway may be disqualified anytime.
  • You are allowed to attend ONLY ONE division.

The 2K and weaker Handicap division players:

  • Must enter with your strongest account. (Contact the user "sweety" if your KGS Plus account is not your strongest account)
  • Have played rated games for at least 6 weeks, and have played at least 30 games before December 31.

Please respect your opponents, and don't ask a Bye when there are less than 8 players around.
